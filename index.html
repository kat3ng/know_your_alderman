What is an alderman?

Each ward in Chicago has an Alderman

There are 50 wards

50 wards = 50 aldermen in Chicago

Terms: 4 years

Aldermen make up City Council

City Council serves as the local legislative branch for the city

Has 19 standing committees

Aldermen have “the power to regulate for the protection of the public health, safety, morals and welfare; to license; to tax; and to incur debt.“
